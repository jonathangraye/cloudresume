Music

Growing up in throughout the 2010's in England, I've always been immersed in music. Music to me is an outlet, a means of communication. It's a way to express how I feel when I don't have words or the ability to communicate otherwise.

I've always gravitated towards intense and emotive music, where loneliness, anger and meloncholia are themes. I take solace in writing, recording, and mixing tracks which I hope you can enjoy.
