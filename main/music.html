Music

Growing up in throughout the 2010's in England, I was always exposed to and immersed in music. Music to me is an outlet, a means of communication, as a way to express how I feel when I don't have words or the ability to communicate otherwise

I've always gravitated towards emotive music which attempts to display emotions like anger and sadness. I always found solace in playing guitar, learning songs written by other great musicians and eventually recording and producing my own music.

I'm still learning, but I hope you have the chance to listen to some of what I've made.
